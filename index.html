About Sameer.

Sameer Paila is a rising senior at Dublin High school, with a passion for speech and debate, economics, and politics. Noticing the hostile climate that now defines American discourse, Sameer reached out to his community in hopes of teaching a more deliberate and respectful debate methodology to the youth. Moving forward, he wants to cultivate a more objective generation by reaching out to as many children within his community as possible. You can find his resume here.
